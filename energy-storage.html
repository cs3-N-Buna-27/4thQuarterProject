Lithium-ion batteries are advancing in energy density, lifespan, and safety, making them more suitable for large-scale energy storage.

Flow batteries provide scalability and a long cycle life, and there is ongoing research to improve their energy density and efficiency.

Hydrogen storage technologies, including fuel cells and electrolysis, are emerging as solutions for long-duration energy storage and transportation.
